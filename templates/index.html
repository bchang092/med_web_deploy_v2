VitalVoices

Your comprehensive hospital volunteer review page, designed to streamline and enhance communication with volunteers for better patient care.

About Us and Our Mission Statement

As a guest ambassador at a local hospital, I serve as a primary point of contact for patients, interacting daily to provide support and address their needs. Through engaging with over a thousand patients, I've gained invaluable insights into their experiences. However, a critical issue persists: a lack of effective communication between volunteers and hospital administration.

This gap is particularly detrimental because patients – especially elderly individuals unfamiliar with online feedback systems – often prefer to share their concerns with the most direct point of contact: volunteers. This valuable feedback often goes unheard, leaving a significant gap in understanding patient needs. This problem is experienced by millions of volunteers nationwide.

Our mission with this website is to bridge this communication gap, establishing a direct line between volunteers and hospital staff. This ensures that both patient concerns shared through volunteers and volunteer feedback are heard, ultimately enhancing patient care and satisfaction.

Volunteer-Admin Communication

We provide a clear communication channel between volunteers and administrators to actively manage and assess patient care, with transparent access to past reviews from both parties.

AI-based Sentiment Analysis

We have developed a custom filter system powered by AI language models to analyze volunteer review sentiments, offering a more accurate assessment of volunteer and patient satisfaction beyond simple ratings.

Comprehensive Graphical Analysis

We have created a comprehensive graphical analysis page that provides a clear breakdown of overall hospital and departmental performance, highlighting areas for improvement and satisfaction.
