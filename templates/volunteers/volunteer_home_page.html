Why Your Voice Matters

Elderly patients, typically those over 60 years old, make up 30-40% of all hospital patients. However, many of these patients struggle to navigate the complex systems hospitals use for feedback submission. As a result, they often prefer to communicate their experiences verbally to someone they trust—like you, the volunteer. Unfortunately, many hospitals lack proper communication channels with volunteers, missing out on an important source of insights that could significantly improve patient care. Your voice provides an unbiased perspective on hospital services, playing an invaluable role in enhancing the overall patient experience.
