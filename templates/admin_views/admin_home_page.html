Our Volunteer Review System

As the admin of this review platform, you play a key role in ensuring that valuable feedback from elderly patients is captured and acted upon. Elderly patients, typically those over 60, make up 30-40% of all hospital patients. However, many of these individuals face difficulties navigating complex systems to submit their feedback. Instead, the often opt to verbally share their experiences to our volunteers.

Many hospitals lack the infrastructure to effectively channel feedback from volunteers, missing out on critical insights that could enhance patient care. This platform is designed to ensure that these insights are collected and analyzed systematically, providing an unbiased perspective on hospital services. We've provided vital analysis tools for you to transform patient experiences by identifying areas of improvement.
